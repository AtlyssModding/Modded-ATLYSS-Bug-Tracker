# PropPlacer Statistics
Statistical analysis of all (non-deprecated) Prop_Placer packs put into a spreadsheet. Note that this is reading off of the PropBundle.\
The information is gathered by checking "Properties" on PropBundles files & Asset folders and reading "Size" instead of "Size on disk".\
"Uncompressed" is the actual* size of the props before being bundled (compressed), the information was obtained using [AssetRipper](https://github.com/AssetRipper/AssetRipper) by exportng primary content.
